% ── 1. MOTIVATION & PROBLEM ───────────────────────────────────
\begin{posterbox}[title=1~~Motivation \& Problem Statement]
\justifying
Agriculture still relies on \textbf{uniform pesticide spraying}---chemicals applied
across the field without considering individual plant health, leading to
\textbf{chemical wastage}, increased cost, soil contamination, and unnecessary
exposure of healthy plants.

\vspace{0.3em}
\begin{resultbox}
\centering\large
\textit{``How can we spray pesticide only where needed\\
and in proportion to disease severity?''}
\end{resultbox}
\vspace{0.3em}

\textbf{Core challenge:} Build a closed-loop system connecting image-based
severity detection with proportional pesticide actuation.

\vspace{0.4em}

% ── 2. RESEARCH GAP ──────────────────────────────────────────
\begin{posterbox}[title=2~~Research Gap]
\justifying
Most existing systems handle detection and spraying \textit{separately}:

\vspace{0.3em}
\begin{itemize}
  \item ML systems detect disease but \textbf{do not control spray quantity}
  \item Robotic sprayers are costly and not severity-aware
  \item Binary infected/not-infected decisions are insufficient
  \item No affordable system links \textbf{detection with proportional actuation}
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.3em}
\begin{resultbox}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.3}
\centering
\begin{tabular}{p{0.38\linewidth}p{0.54\linewidth}}
\toprule
\textbf{Existing Direction} & \textbf{Limitation} \\
\midrule
ML disease detection & Stops at classification \\
Robotic sprayers & Expensive, not severity-aware \\
AI-IoT farming & Limited proportional control \\
\textbf{Our system} & \hlgood{Detection + proportional actuation} \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{resultbox}
\end{posterbox}

\vspace{0.4cm}

% ── 3. OBJECTIVES ────────────────────────────────────────────
\begin{posterbox}[title=3~~Objectives, colbacktitle=AccentBlue]
\justifying
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Capture tomato leaf images using ESP32-CAM module
  \item Classify infection severity: \textit{Healthy\,/\,Moderate\,/\,Severe}
  \item Map severity to calibrated pump actuation duration
  \item Implement microcontroller-based pump control
  \item Display infection status via RGB LED and 7-segment display
  \item Reduce pesticide usage vs.\ conventional uniform spraying
  \item Build a low-cost, working prototype for academic use
\end{enumerate}
\end{posterbox}

\vspace{0.4cm}
